About Jim Haessig

Owner of Riverstone Center for Massage and Well-Being, Jim is a licensed massage therapist and a certified Wellness Coach from the Mayo Clinic. Additionally he is a Core Energetics Practitioner, certified from the Core Energetics Institute of NY. He holds a BS degree from Villanova University. Jim started his career in the commercial printing industry, owning and managing Plains Printers and then MMP Printing Group for 30 years. A lifetime resident of Morris County, Jim is married to Beth and has three sons, Danny, Peter, and TJ.
